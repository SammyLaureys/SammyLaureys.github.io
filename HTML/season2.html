Finding Monty gone from his cell, Clarke breaks out and discovers she is trapped in an underground complex inside Mount Weather. After meeting their leader, President Dante Wallace, Clarke is invited to stay along with the 47 surviving teenagers out of the original 100 that landed on Earth. Distrusting him, she makes a break for freedom but is stopped by Jasper. Clarke secretly plots an escape.
