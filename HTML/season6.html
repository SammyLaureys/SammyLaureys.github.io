Vorige Volgende

Clarke, Bellamy, Echo, Emori, Murphy, Miller, Jackson, and Shaw go down to explore planet Alpha and determine its survivability. While the group is chased by a swarm of native bugs, Shaw runs into a shield of radiation and dies. The others make it to a seemingly abandoned compound. After Bellamy confronts Clarke over the radio calls, he discovers a children’s book depicting the planet’s binary solar eclipse causing people to go insane.
