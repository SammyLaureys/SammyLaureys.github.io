Vorige Volgende

Set in an indeterminate year in the distant future, 97 years after a nuclear apocalypse has devastated the surface of Earth, all known humans are residents of merged orbiting space stations known as "The Ark". 100 juvenile delinquents are sent to Earth's surface to test its habitability, having been given vitals-monitoring wristbands and instructions to proceed directly to Mount Weather . Among them is 17-year-old Clarke Griffin, the daughter of The Ark's chief medical officer, Dr. Abby. The 100 discover a lush Earth filled with new wonders and dangers.
